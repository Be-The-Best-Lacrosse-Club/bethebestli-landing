# BTB Digital Academy Intro Script

## Recording 1: The Problem

Most players train their stick.
They train their feet.
They train their shot.

But the game is moving faster now.
The players who separate are the ones who understand what they are seeing.
They recognize the slide.
They know where the space is.
They make the next decision before everyone else catches up.

That is why we built the BTB Digital Academy.

## Recording 2: The Platform

This is a new layer of development for BTB families.
It brings film, IQ, position work, and player education into one online platform.

Our goal is simple: help players understand the game better, not just work harder.

The Academy is built for boys and girls players who want to keep learning between practices, between tournaments, and through the full season.

It opens next season.
Families who want early access can request it in August.
