What This Is

THE DIGITAL LAYER
BTB HAS BEEN BUILDING TOWARD.

The BTB Digital Academy is our premium online learning platform for lacrosse IQ, film study, positional education, and player development. It is built for boys and girls players who want to keep learning outside field time.

How We Build Players

HOW WE ACTUALLY
BUILD PLAYERS.

01 / 04
SG
Small-Group Coaching

We keep coaching intentional so players get real reps, real corrections, and direct teaching instead of getting lost in line drills.

02 / 04
PLAN
Structured Teaching

BTB sessions are organized around clear teaching points, purposeful reps, and a real development objective. The work is planned, not random.

03 / 04
FILM
Film + IQ

Players study film so the game slows down. We teach reads, spacing, slides, and decisions, not just drills and speeches.

04 / 04
BUILD
Season Build

The goal is connected development across the season: skill work, team concepts, player accountability, and learning that carries into games.
