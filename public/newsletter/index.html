02 · INTERNAL · LIVE

BTB-OS

The all-inclusive, integrated hub our coaching staff operates from every day. Attendance, schedules, communication, and every BTB system — unified into a single platform.

Coaches access a complete library of practice plans, drills, and the ability to build their own personal playbook. Every workflow that used to live in five different places now lives in one.

Attendance
Tracked in real time at every practice and event.
Schedules
Full team and club calendars in one view.
Playbook
Practice plans, drills, and coach-built playbooks.
Automations
SMS updates and reminders sent automatically.
★ Built-In Accountability

Attendance is closely monitored because it directly impacts player development and team performance. Parents receive proactive alerts if their player's attendance puts them at risk of consequences — such as reduced playing time in a tournament — well before any decision is made. The goal is transparency and partnership, not surprise.

For BTB families: more consistent coaching, faster communication, zero information falling through the cracks.

Designed by Coach Dan

Coach Dan has spent the last six months building this — drawing from over twenty years of coaching experience — to create something the travel lacrosse world hasn't seen.

Practice gets you reps. Tournaments get you exposure. But the players who actually get recruited — who play varsity early, start in college, lead programs — are doing the work between practices that nobody sees. The Online Academy delivers that work, year-round, on every player's phone.

A Tiered Curriculum

Knowledge that scales
with the player.

Each age group gets its own track. As players grow, the depth grows with them. Concepts compound year over year — never repeated, always built upon.

01
Foundation Tier
Younger age groups · Game basics, vocabulary, IQ fundamentals.
02
Development Tier
Middle age groups · Position concepts, advanced IQ, situational play.
03
Mastery Tier
High school years · Film study, recruiting prep, college-level concepts.
What's Covered

Four pillars. Every tier.

01

Film Study

Read the game faster. See what coaches see.

02

Lacrosse IQ

Decisions, situations, schemes. The mental game built systematically.

03

Position Training

Attack, middie, defense, goalie — specialized work, not generic drills.

04

Off-Field

Recruiting, character, leadership, accountability. The full athlete.

★ The Wall of Fame

Finish a tier.
Earn your spot.

Every player who completes a tier earns a permanent place on the BTB Wall of Fame — featured directly on bethebestli.com. Public recognition for private work. Proof that the off-field grind is being seen.

The End Goal

The work today is
the recruiting edge tomorrow.

Knowledge compounds. A 2034 player who masters Foundation Tier this year arrives at high school years ahead of peers who skipped it. By the time the recruiting process starts, BTB players have already done the hard work — and it shows.
