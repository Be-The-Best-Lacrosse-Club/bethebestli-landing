TeamSnap Setup

One-Time Authorization • Owner Only

This page connects the BTB website to your TeamSnap account so the Parent Portal's tournament schedule can pull live data. You only need to do this once.

Step 1 — Register a TeamSnap App

  1. Go to auth.teamsnap.com and log in with your BTB TeamSnap account.
  2. Click your name (top right) → Your Applications → New Application.
  3. Fill it in:
    • Name: BTB Website
    • Description: Live tournament schedule for bethebestli.com
    • Redirect URI: https://www.bethebestli.com/.netlify/functions/teamsnap-callback
    • Scopes: read
  4. Submit. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret it gives you.

Step 2 — Add Credentials to Netlify

Go to Netlify → Site settings → Environment variables → Add a variable, and add both:

  • TEAMSNAP_CLIENT_ID = your Client ID
  • TEAMSNAP_CLIENT_SECRET = your Client Secret

Then: Deploys → Trigger deploy → Deploy site. Wait for it to finish before continuing.

Step 3 — Authorize

Paste the Client ID below and click Authorize. You'll be redirected to TeamSnap to approve the connection, then back here with your refresh token.

This is saved in your browser so you don't have to paste it again.

After You Authorize

TeamSnap will send you back to a page that displays your refresh token. Add it to Netlify:

  • TEAMSNAP_REFRESH_TOKEN = the token shown on the callback page

Trigger another deploy. Then visit tournament-schedule.html to confirm the schedule loads.

Troubleshooting

  • "Not configured" error on the schedule page: The three env vars aren't all set. Double-check spelling.
  • "Redirect URI mismatch" on TeamSnap: The URI in the TeamSnap app must match exactly, including https://www. (not bare bethebestli.com).
  • No events showing: Your TeamSnap events need is_game = true and dates in the range. The function fetches May/June/July of the current year by default.
